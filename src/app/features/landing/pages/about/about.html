Our Mission

Why we built
ApnaInsights

Most career and tech content online is written for a Western audience. We built ApnaInsights for India - with Indian salaries, Indian companies, and Indian context.

Written by real professionals, not AI

Every article on ApnaInsights is written by a verified professional with real experience in that field. A software engineer at a Bangalore startup. A recruiter at an MNC. A CA who has filed hundreds of returns. Real people, real knowledge.

Career content built for the Indian job market

How to crack a TCS interview. What to expect at your first Infosys appraisal. How to negotiate a 30% hike in India. How to switch from service to product. We cover the career questions Indian professionals actually have.

Tech guides that work in India

Not another "build a FAANG portfolio" tutorial. We write about the tools, languages, and frameworks that get you hired at Indian tech companies - from Wipro to Zepto - with the salaries and skills that actually matter here.

Contribute your expertise for free

If you know something that can help another Indian professional grow, write it here. A rich editor, SEO-friendly publishing, and a follower system - everything you need to build a reputation and reach readers who care.
