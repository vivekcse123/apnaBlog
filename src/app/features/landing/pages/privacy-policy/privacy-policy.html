1. Information We Collect

Information You Provide

When you register an account or contact us, we may collect:

  • Name and email address (for account registration)
  • Content you publish (blog posts, categories, descriptions)
  • Messages sent via our contact form

Information Collected Automatically

When you visit ApnaInsights, we and our third-party partners automatically collect:

  • IP address and approximate geographic location
  • Browser type and version
  • Pages visited, time spent, referring URLs
  • Device type (mobile, desktop, tablet)
  • Cookies and similar tracking technologies (see Section 4)

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve the ApnaInsights platform
  • Authenticate your account and keep it secure
  • Display your published blog posts to readers
  • Respond to your inquiries and support requests
  • Send transactional emails related to your account (no marketing emails without consent)
  • Analyze traffic patterns to improve user experience
  • Serve relevant advertisements through Google AdSense (see Section 3)
  • Comply with applicable legal obligations

We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

3. Google AdSense & Advertising

ApnaInsights uses Google AdSense to display advertisements. Google AdSense is a service provided by Google LLC that uses cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this and other websites.

Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads to you based on your visit to ApnaInsights and/or other sites on the Internet. You may opt out of personalised advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings.

Key points about Google AdSense:

  • Google may use cookies, web beacons, and similar technologies on our site.
  • Google's advertising partners may also collect data from your visits to third-party websites.
  • Ads may be personalised based on your interests as determined by your browsing history.
  • You can opt out of Google's use of cookies for personalised ads at aboutads.info.
  • Google's full privacy policy is available at policies.google.com/privacy.

Third-party vendors and ad networks may also use cookies to serve ads based on your visits to ApnaInsights. Users may opt out of third-party vendor cookies by visiting the Network Advertising Initiative opt-out page.

4. Cookies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device by your browser. ApnaInsights uses cookies for the following purposes:

Cookie Type Purpose Examples
Essential Required for the site to function (authentication, security) Session tokens, CSRF tokens
Preference Remember your settings (theme preference) Theme (light/dark)
Analytics Understand how visitors use the site Page views, session duration
Advertising Deliver relevant ads via Google AdSense Google DoubleClick, Google Ads cookies

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Note that disabling certain cookies may affect the functionality of ApnaInsights. Most browsers allow you to:

  • View cookies stored on your device
  • Block cookies from specific websites
  • Delete all cookies when you close your browser

Refer to your browser's help documentation for instructions on managing cookies.

6. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you services. Published blog posts remain visible unless you delete them. If you close your account, we will delete or anonymise your personal data within 30 days, except where retention is required by law.

To request deletion of your account and associated data, please email hello@apnainsights.com.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correction: Ask us to correct inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Deletion: Request erasure of your personal data ("right to be forgotten").
  • Objection: Object to processing of your data for marketing or advertising purposes.
  • Portability: Request transfer of your data in a machine-readable format.
  • Opt-out of personalised ads: Manage your ad preferences via Google Ads Settings.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at hello@apnainsights.com. We will respond within 30 days.

8. Children's Privacy

ApnaInsights is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately at hello@apnainsights.com and we will promptly delete it.

9. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information, including:

  • HTTPS encryption for all data transmitted between your browser and our servers
  • Secure password hashing (bcrypt)
  • HTTP security headers (HSTS, CSP, X-Frame-Options) via Helmet.js
  • JWT-based authentication with secure token expiry

No method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your personal information, we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

Continued use of ApnaInsights after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy.
